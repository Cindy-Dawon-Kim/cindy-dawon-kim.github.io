Eddie Zammit
Melbourne, Australia

Eddie Zammit has formed his passion-turned-obsession into a business. Since 2006, he's been publishing T-world, an internationally-distributed journal dedicated to a quintessential item in just about anyone's wardrobe, the T-shirt. He owns so many T-shirts that, in theory, he could wear a different one each day for the next twenty years. He's just turned forty and you'd think he'd be slowing down, but he's busier than ever.
