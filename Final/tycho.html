TYCHO
San Francisco, California

A graphic designer by trade, Scott Hansen was introduced to electronic music production in his early twenties and has effectively combined the two complimentary mediums to create the identity of his musical project, Tycho. Equally recognized for his design work under the alias "ISO50" as he is for his music, Scott has been blurring the lines between audio and visual art for twenty years.
