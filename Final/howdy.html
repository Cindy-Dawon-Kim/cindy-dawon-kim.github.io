Howdy
Long Beach, California

Justin Blanks, who signs his name as Howdy, is a country boy through and through. You can sense it in his style, hear it in his voice, and see it in his artwork. Last year he made the move from small-town Texas to Long Beach, California, where he currently spends his work (and play) time as a designer and illustrator.
