Cryptik
Los Angeles, California

Cryptik's work is a reflection of the man himself: a student of religion, philosophy, and science with no allegiance to any particular school of thought. Although his face has only been seen by a select few, the LA-based artist's signature style of murals have seen by millions around the globe. Using the city of Los Angeles as his primary canvas, Cryptik spreads his message of higher learning with the intention of provoking thought and insight.
