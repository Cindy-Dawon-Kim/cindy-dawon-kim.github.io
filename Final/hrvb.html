HRVB
Berlin, Germany

Growing up in the streets of Berlin, HRVB has been surrounded by graffiti his entire life. It was almost inevitable that this environment would prompt him to pick up his first can of spray paint at the age of thirteen. Two decades later, the renowned illustrator and street artist is consistently pushing his work to new levels, driven by his fear of a normal life and the dreaded nine-to-five routine.
