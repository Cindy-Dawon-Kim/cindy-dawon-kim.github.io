Rik Oostenbroek
Hilversum, Holland

Rik Oostenbroek was born and raised in the cozy town of Hilversum, located a short train ride away from Amsterdam in North Holland. While his hometown may be small, his imagination and his ambition are endless. He became a freelance designer when he was only seventeen, and since then he has worked with some of the biggest brands in the world.
