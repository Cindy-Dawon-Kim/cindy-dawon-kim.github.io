Then&Now is a new personal calendar application for iOS and Android, designed to record the everyday of the user.

Features Include:

  • 5 Different Personalized Themes
  • Daily Notifications
  • Monthly and Daily View
  • Notes
  • Automatic Time Lapse Video Generator
  • Share on Facebook and Twitter
    
Designed by Cindy Dawon Kim
